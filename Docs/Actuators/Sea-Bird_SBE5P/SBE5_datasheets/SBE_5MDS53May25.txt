SBE 5M
MINI SUBMERSIBLE PUMP




Overview
The SBE 5M Mini Submersible Pump is a modular component
used on several Sea-Bird CTD packages. The highly reliable
pump flushes water through the conductivity cell at a constant
rate, independent of the CTD’s motion, improving dynamic
performance. The SBE 5M is standard on the SBE 19plus V2
SeaCAT Profiler CTD, and is optional on the SBE 16plus V2 and
16plus-IM V2 SeaCAT CT(D) Recorders. For applications requiring
pumping through additional sensors (for example, a dissolved
oxygen sensor), use the SBE 5T or 5P pump instead.
The SBE 5M may also be suitable for custom applications.
