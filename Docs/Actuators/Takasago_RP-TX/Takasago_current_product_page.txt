FEATURES / CHARACTERISTICS
This pump features the world's lowest discharge rate class for a peristaltic pump on the market: 0.03 ~ 40 μL/min.
The pump head is replaceable and comes complete with tubing for ease of use.
Compact dimensions of 33 x 12 x 22.8 mm.
An intuitive controller is available separately. Contact us for more details about the controller and its compatibility with RP-TX.
Controllers for RP-TX
Autoclavable pump heads are also available (sold separately). Reach out for more information or to place an order.
