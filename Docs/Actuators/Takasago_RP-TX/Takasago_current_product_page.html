Inquiry / Quotation

FEATURES / CHARACTERISTICS

   

  1. This pump features the world's lowest discharge rate class for a peristaltic pump on the market: 0.03 ~ 40 μL/min.
  2. The pump head is replaceable and comes complete with tubing for ease of use.
  3. Compact dimensions of 33 x 12 x 22.8 mm.
  4. An intuitive controller is available separately. Contact us for more details about the controller and its compatibility with RP-TX.

    Controllers for RP-TX

  5. Autoclavable pump heads are also available (sold separately). Reach out for more information or to place an order.

    Replaceable and Autoclavable Pump Heads
