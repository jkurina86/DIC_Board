SHT35-DIS-B

  • All products
  • •Humidity sensors
  • •SHT3x

±1.5% (0-80%RH) Digital humidity and temperature sensor

The digital SHT3x humidity sensor series takes sensor technology to a new level. As the successor of the SHT2x series it sets the industry standard in humidity sensing. The SHT3x humidity sensor series consists of a low-cost version with the SHT30 humidity sensor, a standard version with the SHT31 humidity sensor, and a high-end version with the SHT35 humidity sensor. Automotive grade versions are also available. The SHT3x humidity sensor series combines multiple functions and various interfaces (I²C, analog voltage output) with a applications-friendly, very wide operating voltage range (2.15 to 5.5 V). The SHT3x humidity sensor is available in both large and small volumes.
