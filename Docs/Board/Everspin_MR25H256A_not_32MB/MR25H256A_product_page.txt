MR25H256 - 256Kb Serial SPI Interface MRAM
The MR25H256 is the ideal memory for applications that must store and retrieve data and programs quickly using a minimum number of pins.
AEC-Q100 Grade 1 Qualified options available.
40MHz Read/Write speed with unlimited endurance.
Data non-volatile with 20 years retention.
Data retained on power loss.
RoHS compliant packages.
